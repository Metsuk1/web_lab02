Aktobe

Aktobe is a dynamic city in the west of Kazakhstan: modern spaces, parks, museums and steppe routes are nearby. Convenient logistics and a welcoming atmosphere.

The city combines modern infrastructure with the traditions of the Kazakh steppe. Here you can explore cultural landmarks, taste local cuisine and experience the hospitality of Western Kazakhstan.

From steppe modernity to heritage

City center & Sazdy embankment

  • Sazdy River Embankment - a walking area with bike paths, a promenade and views of the city.
  • The City Park and Arbat are cozy alleys, outdoor cafes and seasonal fairs in the center of Aktobe.
  • Nurgasyr Central Mosque is a modern religious complex and one of the business cards of the city.

History & heritage

  • Aktobe Regional Museum of Local Lore - archeology, ethnography, and the history of the region's industrial development.
  • The Museum of Aliya Moldagulova is an exhibition about the Hero of the Soviet Union, a native of Aktobe region.
  • St. Nicholas Cathedral is an Orthodox church and an example of historical urban architecture.

Art and Culture

  • Regional Art Gallery - exhibitions of paintings and decorative and applied art by local and invited authors.
  • Concert venues and city Houses of Culture - classical concerts, theatrical productions and festivals.

Sports and events

  • "Koblandy Batyr" Central Stadium is the home arena of FC Aktobe and a venue for major city events.
  • "Konys" Sports Palace - tournaments in game sports, martial arts, mass events.
  • City marathons and bike races are seasonal sports starts along the embankment and central streets.
