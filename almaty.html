From mountain modernity to heritage

City center & scenic viewpoints

  • Kok-Tobe - observation platforms above the city and a cable car from the center.
  • Arbat - main pedestrian street with street performers and cafes.
  • Panfilov Park — a green park in the center.

History & heritage

  • Central State Museum of Kazakhstan is one of the largest museums in the country.
  • The Green Bazaar (Kök Bazaar) is a historic city market and a popular chain of shops.

Art and Culture

  • A. Kasteyev State Museum of Arts is the largest art museum in Kazakhstan.
  • Abai Opera and Ballet Theatre is the city's leading opera house with a classical repertoire.

Sports & Mountain leisure

  • Medeu is a high—altitude skating rink at an altitude of ~1,691 m above sea level.
  • Shymkent — ski resort (≈2260 m), ascent from Medeu by cable car.
  • Almaty Arena / Halyk Arena — multifunctional venues for hockey, concerts and tournaments.
