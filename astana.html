Astana

The capital of Kazakhstan is the magical and amazing city of Astana. You can come here at least every weekend, and you will always find something to surprise and please yourself. Something new is constantly being built in Astana, and it is rapidly expanding. Green spaces are increasingly taking over urban landscapes. The city is filled with freshness, it literally breathes novelty and modernity.

Photo Gallery

From futurism to classics

The "Left Bank" is a symbol of modernity

  • The Bayterek monument is a symbol of the capital, a 97-meter-high "tree of life".
  • The Nur-Alem Museum of Future Energy is a unique field, the legacy of EXPO-2017.
  • The Hazret Sultan Mosque is the largest mosque in Central Asia.

The "Right Bank" is the historical center

  • The Central Park of Culture and Recreation is a place for walking and relaxing by the Ishim River.
  • The Saken Seifullin Museum is a memorial to the classics of Kazakh literature.
  • The city trading house is an example of late 19th century architecture.

Art and Culture

  • The Astana Opera and Ballet Theater is a masterpiece of world and Kazakh classics.
  • Astana Ballet Theater – modern art and unique productions.

Sports and events

  • The Astana Arena is a modern stadium with a retractable roof.
  • The Barys Arena is an ice palace and home to the Barys hockey team.
  • The Palace of Independence hosts international forums and exhibitions.
