Astana City Tour

Explore the Modern Capital of Kazakhstan

About This Tour

The Astana City Tour is a professional sightseeing trip around Kazakhstan's capital city. Experience the blend of modern architecture and rich cultural heritage in one of the world's most rapidly developing capitals.

Our guided tour takes you through the most iconic landmarks and hidden gems of this fascinating city, offering insights into its history and future. Enjoy comfortable transportation and expert guides throughout your journey.

Tour Highlights

Bayterek Tower Experience

Visit the iconic symbol of Astana with panoramic city views from the observation deck and learn about its cultural significance.

Duration: 2 hours
Khan Shatyr Discovery

Explore the world's largest tent structure with shopping, entertainment venues, and the amazing indoor beach complex.

Duration: 3 hours
Hazrat Sultan Mosque

Discover Kazakhstan's largest mosque, an architectural masterpiece blending traditional Islamic and modern design elements.

Duration: 1.5 hours
