Sacred heritage & modern city life

City center & promenades

  • Khoja Ahmed Yasawi Mausoleum - UNESCO World Heritage centerpiece with evening illumination.
  • Keruen-Saray - pedestrian complex with open-air shows, craft courtyards and cafés.
  • Turkistan Arbat - main pedestrian promenade with fountains and street performers.

History & Heritage

  • Azret Sultan State Historical & Cultural Reserve - museum complex around Yasawi’s shrine (Hilvet, Rabia Sultan Begim and more).
  • Turkistan Bazaar - traditional market for spices, dried fruits, sweets and textiles.

Art & Culture

  • Keruen-Saray evening shows - “flying theater”, light & water performances in the courtyard.
  • Regional Drama Theatre & city museums - rotating exhibitions, concerts and cultural events.

Pilgrimage & Day Trips

  • Sauran Fortress - medieval city ruins with segments of walls and gates.
  • Otyrar & Arystan Bab - classic excursion through the Otyrar oasis and the mausoleum of Arystan Bab.
  • Ukash-ata - sacred spring and mausoleum popular with pilgrims.
