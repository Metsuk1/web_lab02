About Turkestan

Turkestan is a Silk Road city centered on the Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi, the Keruen-Saray complex and a walkable old town - heritage and city life in one place.

The city is a cultural heart of southern Kazakhstan, known for its spiritual atmosphere and modern development. Tourists come to admire ancient mausoleums, traditional bazaars, and new architectural projects symbolizing Kazakhstan‚Äôs renewal.

Main Attractions

  • The Mausoleum of Khoja Ahmed Yasawi - the main pilgrimage site in Kazakhstan.
  • Keruen-Saray - cultural and entertainment complex with theaters, museums, and hotels.
  • Rabia Sultan Begim Mausoleum - a historical monument of the Timurid era.

Culture & Traditions

  • Ethno-village "Turkistan" - experience Kazakh crafts and nomadic life.
  • Yasawi University - center of Islamic studies and education.
  • Local Bazaars - traditional food, souvenirs, and handmade goods.

Art and Architecture

  • Regional Art Gallery - showcases traditional and modern Kazakh art.
  • Keruen Theater - open-air performances and cultural shows.
