Almaty Oblysy

Almaty Oblysy is a picturesque region of mountains, canyons and lakes surrounding the city of Almaty. It offers a diverse mix of natural wonders, from snow-capped peaks to desert valleys.

The region includes famous landmarks such as the Charyn Canyon, Kolsai Lakes and Big Almaty Lake. It is a paradise for hikers and travelers who want to see Kazakhstan‚Äôs true beauty - with national parks, glaciers and unique flora and fauna.
