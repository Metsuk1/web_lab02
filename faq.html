Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book a tour? +
You can click on "Explore Now" on our homepage and fill out the reservation form to book your tour.
Can I cancel my booking? +
Yes! You can cancel your booking up to 48 hours before your tour for a full refund.
What should I bring on the tour? +
Comfortable shoes, sunscreen, water, and a camera to capture the beautiful scenery of Kazakhstan.
Are meals included in the tours? +
Some tours include meals; please check the tour description for specific details.
Do I need travel insurance? +
Travel insurance is not mandatory but highly recommended for your safety and peace of mind.
What languages do your guides speak? +
Our guides speak English, Russian, and Kazakh. Some tours also offer French or German on request.
